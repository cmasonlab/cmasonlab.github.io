Meet the researchers behind the Mason Research Lab, past and present.

Principal Investigator
Chandra D. Mason, Ph.D.
Dr. Mason is a critical social-personality-organizational psychologist with more than 25 years of experience conducting research that examines the harms of bias and discrimination on marginalized groups and public attitudes toward justice for disempowered populations. She is Professor of Psychology and Department Chair of Psychological Sciences at Mary Baldwin University, where she directs the Mason Research Lab. Her methodological expertise includes psychometrics, structural equation modeling, path analysis, quasi-experimental methods, and longitudinal designs. Dr. Mason holds a doctorate in Critical Social-Personality Psychology from the CUNY Graduate School and University Center. She has served as an affiliated professor at the University of Virginia and as a consultant to organizations including the Walter Reed Army Institute of Research, Families and Work Institute, Catalyst, The Murphy Deming College of Healh Sciences at MBU, and a Big Four professional services firm.
