Science in service of better living

Specializing in social, personality and organizational psychology research that empowers individuals, organizations and communities.

Serious science.
Passionate inquiry.

The Mason Research Lab applies social psychology to produce rigorous, theory-grounded research that does not just describe the world, but helps improve it. Across every topic we study, our work considers how personal beliefs and social systems, from organizations to educational institutions to emerging technologies, shape people's lives, with attention to the experiences of all groups, including those most often overlooked.

Theoretical rigor
Following Kurt Lewin's dictum that "nothing is so practical as good theory," our work is grounded in social-psychological principles, designed for real-world impact.
Critical perspective
We examine power, bias, and oppression through a critical lens, attending to the experiences of both marginalized and dominant groups.
Powerful data analytics
We ethically and responsibly employ advanced data analytical methods suited to the complex phenomena we study, to arrive at insights that empower and drive meaningful change.
