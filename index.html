Science in service of a more just world

Specializing in critical psychological research that empowers individuals, organizations and communities.

Serious science.
Passionate inquiry.

The Mason Research Lab brings together critical social psychology, organizational behavior, and justice scholarship to produce rigorous, theory-grounded research.

We are committed to producing scholarship that does not just describe the world, but helps change it. Our work centers the experiences of marginalized communities and examines the psychological and structural roots of inequality.

Theoretical rigor
Our work is grounded in social psychological theory and applied to real-world problems.
Critical perspective
We examine power, bias, and oppression through a critical lens, attending to the experiences of both marginalized and dominant groups.
Empowerment through evidence
Research findings translate into tools, frameworks, and insights that empower and drive meaningful change.
