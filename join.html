Join the lab

We are recruiting research assistants for the 2026-2027 academic year. Learn what we look for and how to apply.

Who we are looking for

Research assistants

We welcome applications from undergraduate students who are passionate about social psychology, justice, and evidence-based inquiry. No prior research experience is required, though a genuine curiosity about the questions we pursue is essential.

Strong candidates are reliable, intellectually engaged, and willing to contribute to all phases of the research process, from literature review and data collection to analysis and dissemination.

We are particularly interested in students who share our commitment to research that centers marginalized communities and contributes to social change.

What you will gain

Lab members develop hands-on experience with survey design, data collection and analysis, and academic writing. You will work closely with Dr. Mason and contribute to real ongoing research projects.

Time commitment

We ask for a commitment of approximately 5 to 8 hours per week during the academic year. Lab members are expected to attend regular lab meetings and check in with the PI on assigned tasks.

How to apply

Send an email to Dr. Mason with the subject line "Lab Application" and include a brief statement of interest (one paragraph) and your current resume or CV. We review applications on a rolling basis.
